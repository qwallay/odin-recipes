Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Time for a classic! Chicken noodle soup done quick and easy! A good recipe that doesn't take long and serves up to 6!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans of chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound of chopped cooked chicken breast (rotisserie chicken works too)
  • 1 1/2 cups of egg noodles
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a part pot over medium heat. Add celery and onions and cook until tender.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to boil.
  3. Lower the heat and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  4. Fill a bowl and enjoy!
